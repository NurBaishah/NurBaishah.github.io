About me

I am an undergraduate student at Yonsei University, originally from Malaysia, with a lifelong fascination for computers and technology. From a young age, I was captivated by how software and digital systems work, spending hours learning to code, solve problems, and experiment with creative tech projects.  This early curiosity has grown into a deep passion for computer science, shaping my academic and personal journey.

I aspire to become a computer scientist who not only masters the technical aspects of technology but also leverages it to create meaningful solutions. I thrive on challenges, enjoy exploring new ideas, and am constantly motivated to push the boundaries of what technology can achieve. Whether it’s building innovative applications, designing intuitive digital experiences, or understanding complex algorithms, I am driven by the joy of learning and solving problems.

Beyond the classroom, I love combining creativity and logic to bring ideas to life. From designing apps and interfaces to experimenting with AI and digital projects,  I enjoy projects that merge imagination with technical skill. I am ambitious, curious, and determined to make a tangible impact in the world of technology, turning big ideas into reality and inspiring others along the way.

Research Interest

I am passionate about building intelligent systems that interact naturally with people, whether through physical robots or digital interfaces. I want to explore how artificial intelligence can be applied in real-world contexts to create experiences that are both useful and enjoyable for humans. I am also very interested in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), designing systems that understand and respond to people in intuitive ways, and Multimodal Interaction Design, where technology can interpret and combine different forms of input like speech, gestures, and touch. I also want to focus on Embodied Artificial Intelligence, developing robots and agents that can act and adapt in the physical world, and Learning-Based Control Systems, where machines improve their performance over time through experience. Ultimately, my goal is to create technology that feels intelligent, responsive, and human-aware, bridging the gap between people and machines in ways that are seamless and natural.
